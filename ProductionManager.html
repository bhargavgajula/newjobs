Production Manager

Manufacturing and Production Sector

About the Role

Production Managers are responsible for overseeing the manufacturing process within a plant or factory. They ensure that production runs smoothly, on time, and within budget, while maintaining high standards of quality and safety. They also manage staff, resources, and production schedules.

Key Duties

  • Plan, organize, and oversee production schedules
  • Ensure production meets quality standards and targets
  • Manage and supervise production staff
  • Coordinate with other departments (e.g., procurement, logistics) to optimize production
  • Monitor production processes and implement improvements

Skills Required

  • Strong leadership and management skills
  • Excellent organizational and planning abilities
  • Ability to solve problems and make decisions quickly
  • Knowledge of production processes and quality control standards
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering, Manufacturing, or related field
  • Experience in production management or a related role
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., Certified Production and Inventory Management) are a plus
  • Minimum age: 21 years

Additional Information

  • Salary: $70,000 - $110,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
