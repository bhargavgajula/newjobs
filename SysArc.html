System Architect

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

System Architects are responsible for designing and implementing complex IT systems and infrastructure solutions. They analyze business requirements, evaluate technologies, and develop architectural designs to meet organizational needs.

Key Duties

  • Develop architectural designs and technical specifications.
  • Lead system integration and deployment activities.
  • Provide technical guidance and support to development teams.
  • Ensure system scalability, reliability, and security.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to define system requirements and goals.

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of software architecture principles and design patterns
  • Experience with cloud computing platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure)
  • Proficiency in programming languages (e.g., Java, Python)
  • Understanding of network architecture and protocols
  • Excellent problem-solving and decision-making skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • Proven experience in system architecture and design
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., AWS Certified Solutions Architect)
  • Minimum age: 25 years
  • Strong communication and leadership abilities

Additional Information

  • Salary: $100,000 - $160,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, stock options, professional development opportunities
