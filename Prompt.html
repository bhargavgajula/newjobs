Prompt Engineer

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

A Prompt Engineer is responsible for crafting effective prompts that guide large language models (LLMs) to generate the desired output. They understand the capabilities and limitations of LLMs and use their knowledge of language and AI to create prompts that elicit specific responses. Prompt Engineers play a crucial role in developing and optimizing AI applications.

Key Duties

  • Analyze project requirements and user needs to understand desired LLM outputs
  • Develop and iterate on prompts to elicit specific responses from LLMs
  • Test and evaluate the effectiveness of prompts in generating accurate and relevant outputs
  • Stay up-to-date on advancements in large language models and prompt engineering techniques
  • Collaborate with AI researchers, developers, and data scientists to optimize LLM performance
  • Document and share best practices for prompt engineering within the team

Skills Required

  • Strong understanding of natural language processing (NLP) and artificial intelligence (AI)
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to analyze complex information and identify patterns
  • Experience with large language models (LLMs) and generative AI techniques
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Experience with programming languages (e.g., Python) a plus

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Master's degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or related field (preferred)
  • Experience working with large language models or generative AI
  • Strong understanding of natural language processing (NLP) concepts
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
