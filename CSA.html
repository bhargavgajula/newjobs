Cloud Solution Architect

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

Cloud Solution Architects are responsible for designing and implementing cloud-based solutions and architectures that meet business and technical requirements. They work with stakeholders to define cloud strategies and select appropriate cloud services.

Key Duties

  • Design cloud architectures that meet scalability, reliability, and security requirements.
  • Identify and select appropriate cloud services and technologies (e.g., AWS, Azure).
  • Develop migration plans for moving applications and workloads to the cloud.
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory and security standards.
  • Provide technical guidance and support to development teams.

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of cloud computing principles and architectures
  • Experience with cloud platforms and services (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Understanding of networking, security, and compliance in cloud environments
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Effective communication and stakeholder management abilities

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • Certifications in cloud technologies (e.g., AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Azure Solutions Architect) are preferred
  • Experience in cloud architecture or related roles
  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment

Additional Information

  • Salary: $100,000 - $160,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Flexible, depending on project deadlines
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
