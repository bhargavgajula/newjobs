Robotics Engineer

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

Robotics Engineers are responsible for designing, building, and testing robotic systems and devices. They work on a wide range of projects, from industrial robots for manufacturing to autonomous vehicles and drones for various applications.

Key Duties

  • Design and develop robotic systems and components.
  • Program and test robot behaviors and functionalities.
  • Integrate sensors, actuators, and control systems into robotic platforms.
  • Optimize robot performance and reliability through experimentation and analysis.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to solve complex engineering challenges.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in programming languages (e.g., C++, Python)
  • Knowledge of robotics concepts and principles (e.g., kinematics, dynamics)
  • Experience with robotic simulation and modeling software (e.g., ROS, Gazebo)
  • Understanding of mechatronics and control systems
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Robotics, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or related field
  • Experience in robotics engineering or related roles
  • Proficiency in CAD software for mechanical design (e.g., SolidWorks, AutoCAD)
  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment

Additional Information

  • Salary: $80,000 - $140,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Flexible, depending on project deadlines
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
