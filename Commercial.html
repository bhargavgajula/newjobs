Commercial Banker

Banking Sector

About the Role

Commercial Bankers are responsible for managing and growing the financial portfolios of business clients. They provide a range of services, including loans, credit, and treasury management, to help businesses manage their finances and achieve their goals.

Key Duties

  • Develop and maintain relationships with business clients
  • Assess clients' financial needs and recommend appropriate financial products
  • Analyze financial statements and credit reports to evaluate loan applications
  • Monitor clients' financial performance and manage risks
  • Ensure compliance with banking regulations and policies

Skills Required

  • Strong analytical and financial analysis skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Knowledge of banking products and services
  • Ability to build and maintain client relationships
  • Problem-solving and decision-making abilities

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Business Administration, or related field
  • Experience in commercial banking or related roles
  • Understanding of financial regulations and risk management
  • Minimum age: 21 years

Additional Information

  • Salary: $70,000 - $120,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
