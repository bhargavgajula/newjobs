Sales Account Executive

Sales and Marketing Sector

About the Role

Sales Account Executives are responsible for managing client accounts, building and maintaining strong relationships with clients, and identifying new sales opportunities. They work to meet sales targets and contribute to the overall growth of the business.

Key Duties

  • Manage client accounts and ensure client satisfaction
  • Identify and pursue new sales opportunities
  • Present and demonstrate products/services to clients
  • Negotiate contracts and close sales deals
  • Collaborate with internal teams to meet client needs

Skills Required

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong sales and negotiation skills
  • Ability to build and maintain client relationships
  • Proficiency in CRM software
  • Goal-oriented and self-motivated

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or related field
  • Experience in sales or account management roles
  • Understanding of sales processes and strategies
  • Minimum age: 21 years

Additional Information

  • Salary: $60,000 - $110,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
