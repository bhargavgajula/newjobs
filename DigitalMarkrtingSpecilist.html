Digital Marketing Specialist

Sales and Marketing Sector

About the Role

Digital Marketing Specialists are responsible for developing, implementing, and managing marketing campaigns that promote a company's products or services. They play a major role in enhancing brand awareness within the digital space as well as driving website traffic and acquiring leads/customers.

Key Duties

  • Develop and execute digital marketing campaigns
  • Manage social media platforms and content
  • Analyze website traffic and metrics using tools like Google Analytics
  • Optimize content for search engines (SEO)
  • Coordinate with the design and content teams to create marketing materials

Skills Required

  • Strong understanding of digital marketing channels and strategies
  • Proficiency in tools like Google Analytics, AdWords, and SEO tools
  • Excellent communication and writing skills
  • Creativity and problem-solving abilities
  • Ability to analyze data and derive actionable insights

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field
  • Experience in digital marketing or related roles
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., Google Analytics, AdWords) are a plus
  • Minimum age: 21 years

Additional Information

  • Salary: $50,000 - $90,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
