Innovation Manager

R&D Sector

About the Role

Innovation Managers are responsible for driving and managing the process of innovation within an organization. They identify new opportunities for product development, process improvements, and market expansion. They collaborate with various departments to implement innovative strategies and solutions.

Key Duties

  • Develop and manage the innovation strategy
  • Identify and evaluate new opportunities for innovation
  • Lead cross-functional teams to implement innovative projects
  • Monitor industry trends and competitor activities
  • Promote a culture of innovation within the organization

Skills Required

  • Strong strategic thinking and analytical skills
  • Excellent project management and leadership abilities
  • Creativity and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams
  • Proficiency in market research and data analysis

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Innovation Management, or related field
  • Experience in innovation management or related roles
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., Certified Innovation Leader) are a plus
  • Minimum age: 21 years

Additional Information

  • Salary: $80,000 - $150,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
