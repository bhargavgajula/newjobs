Loan Recovery Agent

Banking Sector

About the Role

Loan Recovery Agents are responsible for contacting borrowers with delinquent loan accounts to negotiate repayment plans and recover outstanding debts. They work to resolve overdue payments while maintaining a professional and empathetic approach.

Key Duties

  • Contact borrowers to discuss overdue payments and negotiate repayment plans
  • Review and analyze borrowers' financial situations
  • Document all communications and payment agreements
  • Work with legal teams to initiate recovery actions if necessary
  • Provide excellent customer service while maintaining professionalism

Skills Required

  • Strong communication and negotiation skills
  • Empathy and patience when dealing with borrowers
  • Knowledge of debt collection laws and regulations
  • Attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Problem-solving and conflict resolution abilities

Qualifications and Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent; bachelor's degree preferred
  • Experience in debt collection or customer service roles
  • Understanding of banking products and services
  • Minimum age: 21 years

Additional Information

  • Salary: $40,000 - $70,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
