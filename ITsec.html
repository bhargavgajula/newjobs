IT Security Specialist

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

An IT Security Specialist is responsible for safeguarding an organization's computer systems and networks from cyber threats. They implement security measures, monitor for vulnerabilities, and respond to security incidents. IT Security Specialists play a vital role in protecting sensitive data and ensuring the smooth operation of IT infrastructure.

Key Duties

  • Design, implement, and maintain security controls to protect against cyberattacks (e.g., firewalls, intrusion detection systems)
  • Identify and assess security vulnerabilities in systems and networks
  • Develop and implement security policies and procedures
  • Monitor security logs and events for suspicious activity
  • Respond to security incidents and take necessary steps to mitigate damage
  • Stay up-to-date on the latest cyber threats and security best practices
  • Educate employees on cybersecurity awareness and best practices

Skills Required

  • Strong understanding of cybersecurity concepts and threats
  • Knowledge of network security protocols and firewalls
  • Experience with security tools and technologies (e.g., intrusion detection systems, vulnerability scanners)
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (preferred)
  • 2-3 years of experience in IT security or a related field
  • Certifications in security best practices (e.g., Security+, CISSP) a plus
  • Experience with security information and event management (SIEM) systems a plus

Additional Information

  • Salary: $70,000 - $110,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with on-call availability for security incidents
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, stock options, professional development opportunities
