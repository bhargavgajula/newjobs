IT Project Manager

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

IT Project Managers oversee the planning, implementation, and completion of IT projects within an organization. They coordinate resources, schedules, and activities to ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget.

Key Duties

  • Develop project plans, goals, and budgets
  • Coordinate resources and activities across multiple teams
  • Monitor project progress and manage risks
  • Communicate project status to stakeholders and management
  • Ensure project deliverables meet quality standards

Skills Required

  • Strong project management skills
  • Excellent communication and leadership skills
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Knowledge of software development lifecycle (SDLC)
  • Problem-solving and decision-making abilities

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • Project management certification (e.g., PMP, PRINCE2) is preferred
  • Experience managing IT projects from initiation to completion
  • Minimum age: 21 years

Additional Information

  • Salary: $90,000 - $140,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
