AI Engineer

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

AI Engineers are responsible for developing, programming, and training complex networks of algorithms that make up AI, so that they can function like a human brain. They work on transforming machine learning models into APIs that other applications can interact with.

Key Duties

  • Design and develop AI models and algorithms.
  • Implement machine learning algorithms and tools.
  • Analyze and interpret complex data.
  • Collaborate with data scientists and software engineers.
  • Maintain and improve existing AI systems.

Skills Required

  • Programming languages: Python, R, Java
  • Experience with machine learning frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch
  • Data analysis and visualization
  • Understanding of neural networks
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Minimum Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, AI, Machine Learning, or related field (Master's preferred)
  • Relevant certifications in AI or machine learning
  • 3-5 projects of experience in AI development
  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities

Additional Information

  • Salary: $80,000 - $130,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, stock options, professional development opportunities
