Big Data Engineer

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

A Big Data Engineer designs, develops, and maintains the infrastructure required to collect, store, process, and analyze large and complex datasets. They work with big data frameworks and tools to ensure efficient data management and retrieval. Big Data Engineers play a crucial role in enabling data-driven decision making and unlocking insights from vast amounts of information.

Key Duties

  • Design and implement big data architectures using frameworks like Apache Hadoop, Spark, or Kafka
  • Develop and manage data pipelines for data ingestion, processing, and storage
  • Optimize big data processing for performance and scalability
  • Work with data analysts and scientists to understand data requirements
  • Troubleshoot and debug big data processing issues
  • Stay up-to-date on advancements in big data technologies and tools

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of big data frameworks (e.g., Hadoop, Spark, Kafka)
  • Experience with distributed computing systems and data warehousing concepts
  • Proficiency in programming languages like Java, Python, or Scala
  • Experience with SQL and NoSQL databases
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (preferred)
  • 3-5 years of experience in big data engineering or related field
  • Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP) a plus
  • Strong understanding of data security and privacy practices

Additional Information

  • Salary: $80,000 - $140,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Typically standard business hours, with flexibility as needed
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, stock options, professional development opportunities
