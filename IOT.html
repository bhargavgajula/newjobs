IoT Developer

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

IoT Developers are responsible for designing, developing, and implementing software applications and solutions for Internet of Things (IoT) devices and systems. They work with hardware and software technologies to create connected and intelligent IoT solutions.

Key Duties

  • Design and develop IoT applications and firmware for embedded systems.
  • Integrate sensors, actuators, and communication modules into IoT devices.
  • Implement data collection, processing, and analysis capabilities.
  • Develop user interfaces and dashboards for IoT solutions.
  • Test and deploy IoT applications in real-world environments.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in programming languages (e.g., C, C++, Python)
  • Experience with embedded systems development
  • Knowledge of IoT protocols and standards (e.g., MQTT, CoAP)
  • Understanding of cloud computing and edge computing concepts
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • Experience in IoT development or related roles
  • Experience with IoT platforms and frameworks
  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment

Additional Information

  • Salary: $90,000 - $150,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Flexible, depending on project deadlines
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
