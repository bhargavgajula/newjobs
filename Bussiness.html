Business Analyst

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

Business Analysts are responsible for analyzing business processes, identifying opportunities for improvement, and recommending solutions to enhance efficiency and productivity. They bridge the gap between business objectives and IT capabilities.

Key Duties

  • Gather and analyze business requirements from stakeholders.
  • Document and communicate requirements to development teams.
  • Identify business problems and propose solutions.
  • Conduct data analysis and create reports and dashboards.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to define project scope and objectives.

Skills Required

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Experience with business analysis tools and techniques
  • Knowledge of software development lifecycle (SDLC)
  • Ability to translate business requirements into technical specifications

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business Administration, Computer Science, or related field
  • Certifications in business analysis (e.g., CBAP) are preferred
  • Experience in business analysis or related roles
  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Strong organizational and time management skills

Additional Information

  • Salary: $70,000 - $120,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
