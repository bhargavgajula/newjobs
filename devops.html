DevOps (Automation) Engineer

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

DevOps Engineers are responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining automation solutions for software development, deployment, and operations. They work with development and operations teams to streamline processes and improve efficiency.

Key Duties

  • Design and implement CI/CD pipelines for automated software delivery.
  • Automate infrastructure provisioning and configuration using tools like Terraform and Ansible.
  • Monitor and optimize system performance and resource utilization.
  • Implement and manage containerization technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Collaborate with development teams to ensure code quality and reliability.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in scripting and programming languages (e.g., Python, Bash)
  • Experience with CI/CD tools (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD)
  • Knowledge of infrastructure as code (IaC) principles and tools
  • Understanding of cloud platforms and services (e.g., AWS, Azure)
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • Experience in DevOps or related roles
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., AWS Certified DevOps Engineer, Certified Kubernetes Administrator) are preferred
  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment

Additional Information

  • Salary: $90,000 - $150,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Flexible, depending on project deadlines
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, professional development opportunities
