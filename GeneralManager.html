General Manager

Hospitality Sector

About the Role

A General Manager in the hospitality sector is the driving force behind the entire operation. They are responsible for the overall success of the establishment, overseeing all departments and ensuring a seamless guest experience. General Managers wear many hats, from managing finances and marketing to leading staff and setting strategic direction.

Key Duties

  • Develop and implement strategic plans to achieve financial and operational goals
  • Oversee all departments within the hospitality establishment (e.g., housekeeping, food and beverage, front desk, sales and marketing)
  • Recruit, hire, train, and supervise department managers
  • Manage financial budgets and ensure profitability
  • Develop and implement marketing and sales strategies to attract guests and boost revenue
  • Maintain high standards for customer service and guest satisfaction
  • Ensure compliance with all health and safety regulations
  • Represent the establishment in the community and build relationships with key stakeholders
  • Analyze performance metrics and identify areas for improvement
  • Stay informed about industry trends and adapt to changing market conditions

Skills Required

  • Strong leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills
  • Excellent strategic thinking and decision-making abilities
  • Proficiency in business management principles and hospitality operations
  • Strong financial management and budgeting skills
  • Experience in marketing and sales (a plus)
  • Ability to build and maintain strong relationships with staff and guests
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently and under pressure
  • Passion for the hospitality industry and a commitment to guest satisfaction

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Hospitality Management, Business Administration, or a related field (preferred)
  • Minimum 5-7 years of experience in hospitality management, with experience in a leadership role
  • Proven track record of success in achieving financial and operational goals
  • Strong leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills
  • Excellent business acumen and financial management skills
  • Experience working in the specific hospitality sector (e.g., hotels, resorts, restaurants) (a plus)
  • Certification in hospitality management (e.g., CHA) (a plus)

Additional Information

  • Salary: $70,000 - $150,000+ per year, depending on experience, location, and size of the establishment
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Long hours, including evenings, weekends, and holidays may be required
  • Location: Hotels, resorts, restaurants, cruise lines, and other hospitality establishments
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, and other benefits may vary by employer
