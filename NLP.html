NLP Engineer

IT and Software Sector

About the Role

NLP Engineers specialize in developing algorithms and models that enable computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language. They work on a variety of applications such as chatbots, sentiment analysis, and language translation.

Key Duties

  • Develop and optimize NLP models and algorithms.
  • Preprocess and analyze large datasets.
  • Implement machine learning techniques for natural language understanding.
  • Collaborate with data scientists and linguists.
  • Deploy NLP models into production environments.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in Python, R, or similar programming languages
  • Experience with NLP libraries: NLTK, SpaCy, Hugging Face Transformers
  • Strong understanding of linguistics and language structure
  • Experience with machine learning frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Minimum Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Linguistics, AI, or related field (Master's preferred)
  • Relevant certifications in NLP or data science
  • 3-5 years of experience in NLP development
  • Minimum age: 25 years
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills

Additional Information

  • Salary: $85,000 - $135,000 per year, depending on experience and qualifications
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM
  • Location: On-site or remote, depending on company policies
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, stock options, professional development opportunities
