Event Manager

Hospitality Sector

About the Role

An Event Manager in the hospitality sector plays a pivotal role in creating memorable experiences for guests. They plan, organize, and execute a wide range of events, from conferences and weddings to social gatherings and product launches. They collaborate with various departments within the hospitality establishment, such as catering, marketing, and guest services, to ensure a flawless event experience.

Key Duties

  • Develop and manage event concepts and proposals based on client needs and budget
  • Secure venues and negotiate contracts with vendors (e.g., caterers, decorators, entertainment)
  • Coordinate logistics such as catering, audio/visual equipment, and guest transportation
  • Develop and manage event budgets and track expenses
  • Oversee event setup, execution, and breakdown
  • Manage event staff and volunteers
  • Ensure adherence to health and safety regulations
  • Market and promote events to attract attendees
  • Gather feedback and evaluate event success

Skills Required

  • Strong organizational, planning, and time management skills
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, and negotiation skills
  • Creativity and a keen eye for detail
  • Budgeting and financial management skills
  • Ability to multi-task and work under pressure
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Experience in the hospitality industry (a plus)
  • Proficiency in event management software (a plus)
  • Excellent customer service skills

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Hospitality Management, Event Management, or a related field (preferred)
  • Experience in event planning or a related role (minimum 2-3 years)
  • Strong organizational, planning, and time management skills
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, and negotiation skills
  • Proven ability to manage and execute events successfully
  • Experience in the hospitality industry (a plus)
  • Certification in Event Management (e.g., CMP) (a plus)

Additional Information

  • Salary: $45,000 - $80,000+ per year, depending on experience, location, and type of hospitality establishment
  • Job Type: Full-time
  • Work Hours: Varied hours, may include evenings, weekends, and holidays depending on event schedule
  • Location: Hotels, resorts, conference centers, event venues, and cruise lines
  • Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plans, and other benefits may vary by employer
